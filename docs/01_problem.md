# Problem Statement - WorkPilot

## Main Problem

Property management companies and housing estates struggle with repetitive communication tasks.

---

## Daily Problems

- Residents ask the same questions repeatedly
- Staff are overloaded with LINE / Facebook messages
- Maintenance requests are lost or delayed
- No centralized system for communication
- Important announcements are missed

---

## Impact

- Staff waste hours answering repetitive questions
- Residents feel ignored or delayed
- Maintenance issues are not tracked properly
- Management has no clear visibility of workload

---

## Core Insight

Most of the work is NOT complex.

It is repetitive communication that can be automated.

---

## Opportunity

If AI can handle:

- Questions
- Requests
- Ticket creation
- Notifications

Then one AI Receptionist can replace a large portion of admin workload.

---

## Why Now

- AI is now good enough to understand Thai language
- Businesses already use LINE/Facebook for communication
- No proper AI receptionist system exists for this market yet
